All Mods & Modules

Includes core Cloud Client modules plus exclusive FrostClient enhancements. Note: The client itself no longer receives updates for mods, but other services and tools remain up-to-date.

  • Nickhider Module
  • Crosshair Module with presets
  • Scroll Zoom & Camera adjustments
  • Nametag, Scoreboard, Bossbar mods
  • Directional HUD & Fireheight options
  • Minimal View Bobbing

  • Custom HUDs and QoL modules exclusive to FrostClient
