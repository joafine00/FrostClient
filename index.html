FrostClient Classic 1.8.9

Welcome to FrostClient Classic

FrostClient Classic is an open-source Minecraft 1.8.9 PvP client designed for competitive gameplay. Lightweight, modular, and optimized for FPS.

About FrostClient

FrostClient Classic combines visual polish, deep mod integration, and open-source transparency.

GitHub: Open Repository

Features

  • Ultra-smooth performance and high FPS optimization
  • Modular design with easy toggles and keybinds
  • Custom HUD editor and cosmetics
  • 1.7 animations, motion blur, replay module, etc.

Included Mods

  • Keystrokes, CPS, FPS Display
  • Armor Status, Coordinates, Zoom
  • Motion Blur, Animations, Hit Color
