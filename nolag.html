FrostClient Beta 1.2.4

What Happened to the Beta Version?

The Beta version 1.2.4 of FrostClient was an important experimental release that focused on performance and bug fixes. During this stage, many issues were resolved, leading to smoother gameplay and higher frame rates for both casual and competitive players.

Players discovered that combining FrostClient with the optimization software ExitLag could drastically increase FPS, even during the free trial period (3–5 days).

After the trial ends, users can still use ExitLag’s Boost feature to optimize their PC’s performance, helping Minecraft run much faster even without active routes for online games (which require a paid plan).

Performance Results

With proper optimization:

  • Low-end PCs can reach up to 900 FPS in Minecraft.
  • High-end setups can reach an average of 1200 FPS.

These improvements were made possible thanks to better system memory handling, CPU balancing, and GPU rendering tweaks introduced in the FrostClient 1.2.4 Beta update.
