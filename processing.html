FrostClient Skin Update & Verification Flow

FrostClient Website Skin Update Process

1. Skins Data from GitHub API
~2 days
2. Processing via Vercel Servers
~3 days
3. Downloading Skins on FrostClient Servers
~5 days
4. Multiple Verification & Antivirus Checks
~6 days
5. Transfer to FrostClient Central Database
~3 days
6. Staff Review & Final Approval
~5 days
7. Skin Published on FrostClient Website
~1 day

FrostClient Minecraft App Skin Update Process

1. Direct API Skin Pull
~1 day
2. Skin Data Added to FrostClient App Database
~1 day
3. Skin Displayed in Minecraft Client
Instant
Note:

• The FrostClient website process is complex and involves many steps of verification, antivirus scanning, and manual staff review to ensure skin authenticity and quality.

• Network latency between GitHub, Vercel, FrostClient servers (located in the US, Portugal, Brazil, and Canada), and the distributed nature of open-source infrastructure contribute to long update times.

• Skins sometimes arrive corrupted or fail to display properly due to latency issues, server downtime, or image corruption during transfers.

• Estimated time for 100 skins to appear fully verified on the website is about 1 week.

• Full synchronization and update of all skin and account data across all servers takes approximately 1 month.

• The FrostClient Minecraft app skips manual verification and pulls skins directly from the API, providing faster updates but with less verification.

• Variations in network speed, server uptime, and geographical distance mean these times can vary widely.
