FrostClient Skin Processing Flow

1. API Global Database (USA Servers)
~4 days
2. Servers Download Skins (Portugal, Brazil, Canada)
~7 days
3. Transfer to Central Database
~5 days
4. Antivirus & Quality Check
~3 days
5. Transfer to FrostClient Database
~4 days
6. Staff Verification & Approval
~5 days
7. Skin Displayed on FrostClient Website
~1 day
Important Notes:
• Due to the distributed nature of servers located in the United States, Portugal, Brazil, and Canada, and the use of open-source code on non-official servers, updating and syncing skin data takes significant time.

• Displaying approximately 100 verified skins on the FrostClient website can take about 1 week due to download and processing delays.

• Complete synchronization of all skin and account data across all servers takes a minimum of 1 month, given network latencies, proxy usage, and server port limitations.

• The FrostClient Minecraft app bypasses manual staff verification and pulls skins directly from the API and FrostClient database, providing faster but less verified skin data.

• These timings are estimations and can vary due to network conditions, server load, and geographic latency.
